Vanilla Cake

This is a simple chocolate cake.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar packed
  • 1 cup cocoa powder Hershey's
  • 1 ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup hot coffee or hot water
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 335ºF and prepare two 9” cake pans with cake goop or another preferred pan release
  2. Combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in the bowl of your stand mixer with the paddle attachment.Mix on low 5 seconds just until combined.
  3. Add in the eggs, buttermilk, oil and vanilla and mix on medium speed for 2 minutes. Scrape the bowl.
  4. Turn the mixer down to low and add in the hot coffee and mix until just its just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into your prepared cake pans.
  6. Bake the cakes for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool the cakes in the pan for about 15 minutes (or until the pan is barely warm to the touch) then invert onto wire racks to cool completely before frosting.
